Why we do what we do

A group of locals started 'Come and Eat!' when the struggle to find motivation & inspiration to cook food after a long day of hard work became too much.

We get it- finding time to feed yourself a warm homemade meal can be hard sometimes. That's why we've created 'Come and Eat!', a community cooking events group, to take the pain of choosing your meals and sourcing the ingredients (while giving you a chance to make some great personal connections) for just Â£5 a session!

Everyone is welcome! From all ages, backgrounds and dietary requirements. We do our best to provide everyone with tasty ingredient substitutions to help fit your needs.
We are always open to improving our processes to ensure everybody's experience is amazing. Community is what makes us great, so just pop your suggestions in the message box while you sign up for a session and we'll come back to you within 48 hours.

All you have to do is decide on the meal(s) you'd like to try cooking out of our four options for the month, sign up and show up. It's that easy.
